## Pro Tips

1. **Batch Content Creation**
   - Create 1 week of content in one sitting
   - Use scheduling tools (Buffer, Hootsuite)
   - Repurpose content across platforms

2. **Engagement Strategy**
   - Respond to all comments within 1 hour
   - Ask questions to encourage replies
   - Use polls and surveys

3. **A/B Testing**
   - Test different headlines
   - Test different images
   - Test different posting times
   - Double down on what works

4. **User-Generated Content**
   - Encourage users to share screenshots
   - Repost customer stories (with permission)
   - Create hashtag campaign (#MyStockAlert)

5. **Consistency is Key**
   - Post at same times daily
   - Maintain brand voice
   - Keep showing up, even when growth is slow
