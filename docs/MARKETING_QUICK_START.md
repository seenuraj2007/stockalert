## Week 3: Content Marketing (Days 15-21)

### Social Media
**Post Daily on:**
- Facebook: Tips, features, customer stories
- LinkedIn: Industry insights, blog posts
- YouTube: Tutorials (1 per week)

**Content Calendar:**
- Monday: Inventory tip
- Tuesday: Blog post share
- Wednesday: Video tutorial
- Thursday: Feature highlight
- Friday: Customer story/testimonial
- Saturday: Industry news
- Sunday: Ask a question/poll

### Start Email Marketing
- [ ] Set up Mailchimp (free tier)
- [ ] Create welcome email series (3 emails)
- [ ] Email 1: Welcome + Quick start guide
- [ ] Email 2: How to create your first invoice
- [ ] Email 3: Advanced features tour

## Week 4: Community & Partnerships (Days 22-30)

### Build Initial Community
- [ ] Join 10 Facebook groups (retail, SMB, inventory management)
- [ ] Engage (don't spam) - answer questions, provide value
- [ ] Share your blog posts when relevant
- [ ] Join Reddit: r/smallbusiness, r/entrepreneur

### Reach Out to Potential Partners
**Create list of:**
- 20 Tally consultants in your city
- 5 accounting firms
- 3 industry associations

**Email template:**
```
Subject: Partnership opportunity for [Their Business]

Hi [Name],

I'm building DKS StockAlert, a free inventory management software for Indian SMBs. 
Many of your clients who use Tally need better inventory tracking.

Would you be interested in:
- 20% commission on referrals
- Co-branded marketing materials
- Free training for your clients

Let's chat for 15 minutes? [Calendly link]

Best,
[Your name]
```

### Launch Referral Program
- [ ] Add "Invite Friends" button in app
- [ ] Offer: Both get 1 month free premium
- [ ] Track with unique referral codes

## What NOT to Do ‚ùå

1. **Don't wait for perfection** - Launch now, improve later
2. **Don't spam** - Provide value first, promote second
3. **Don't ignore metrics** - Track everything, optimize based on data
4. **Don't give up in Week 1** - Marketing takes time
5. **Don't spread too thin** - Master 2 channels before adding more
